About LendIn


Our vision is to provide an institution that delivers financial lending services to people deemed not worth the investment by banks and other NBFIs. These people are dreamers who dream big in their start-ups, SMEs and other enterprises and become the next big story


We provide big investment opportunities with diversified risk to investors who want to expand to something new. Something that provides them with more return for their investment than the traditional banks, stock markets, bond markets or mutual funds. We want them to experience the new way of investment in the 7th biggest economy in the world


Borrowers


We pride ourselves in provinding loans to people who generally aren't thought to be investment worthy by the big greedy corporate banks and NBFIs. And that's a fact


All our member borrowers go through the standard KYC norms as prescribed by the RBI for the banks and lending NBFIs. In addition to that, we may ask for Adhaar details to facilitate the digital signing of the loan deed. The financial details are also used to set up a max ceiling on the interest rate that a borrower can obtain from the LendIn marketplace


Lenders


Our lenders are always looking for a new investment opportunity. They have an inherent advantage by using our platform and our tailormade suggestions for their investment opportunities. We have created a niche market of investors who get some of the best deals in investment out there


All our member lenders go through the standard KYC norms as prescribed by the RBI for the banks and lending NBFIs. In addition to that, we may ask for Adhaar details to facilitate the digital signing of the loan deed.
